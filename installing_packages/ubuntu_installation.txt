-> Take a pendrive(atleast 2 GB) and format it.
-> Download latest ubuntu iso image(17.04) 
-> Open startup disk creator(on Ubuntu) and burn the downloaded iso file on the pendrive.
-> Insert the pendrive and restart your system(read next point before doing this)
-> while booting press f12 to enter boot menu and choose USB.

OS -> Ubuntu 17.04
Free space on Disk -> 291.8 GB / 320 GB
Time taken -> 20 to 25 minutes
